About Me

Welcome! My name is Meghan Yambrick. I grew up in the mountains of West Virginia, and at the age of 18, I made the move to Charlotte to begin a career in Culinary Arts. Well, unfortunately, I ended up hating it. After some years of soul searching, I discovered Web Development. From that moment on, I knew I wanted to begin a career focusing on Front-Line Development.

At the moment, I'm currently learning to code HTML, CSS, JS, and Boostrap. Although I'm still a novice in the field, I am a master of Googling any issue until I can find a solution. Whenever I'm not working on my computer, I enjoy hiking with my dog, cooking, painting, and playing video games.
